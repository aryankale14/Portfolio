# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- Tech recruiters and engineering managers looking to hire an AI/frontend engineer who can deliver high-quality production code.
- Potential freelance clients looking for professional AI solutions and consulting.
- The developer community looking for high-quality open-source tools, code patterns, and project showcases.

## Product Purpose
The portfolio website exists to showcase the work, expertise, and technical craft of Aaryan Kale as an AI Engineer. Success looks like visitors understanding his capabilities within 10 seconds, feeling engaged by the professional aesthetic, and initiating contact for employment, projects, or collaborations.

## Brand Personality
- **Clean**: Meticulous attention to alignment, white space, and visual rhythm.
- **Professional**: Serious, high-performance vibe, signaling capability and expertise.
- **Forward-looking**: Incorporates subtle details that suggest cutting-edge AI engineering (data visualisations, clean typography, premium animations) without crossing into over-styled hype.

## Anti-references
- **Generic AI Template Slop**: Overused purple-to-blue gradients, identical card grids with flat icons, flat typographic hierarchy, or card-in-card nesting.
- **Dry/Academic Resume**: Plain text layouts that lack visual polish, motion design, or interactive feedback.
- **Overwhelming/Flashy**: Avoid excessive, distracting animations, heavy graphics, or slow page load times.

## Design Principles
- **Showcase through Craft**: Let the UI be the proof of engineering capability. Every transition, load state, and interactive element should feel solid and performant.
- **Intentional Density**: Present dense information clearly rather than hiding it behind vague cards, allowing recruiters to quickly find what they need.
- **Micro-Delight**: Use subtle micro-interactions (e.g. precise hover transitions, status indicator pulses, typography clamping) to show attention to detail.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliance: Text contrast >= 4.5:1 for body copy.
- Keyboard navigability: Focus styles on interactive elements.
- Responsive layout: Flawless layout scaling on mobile, tablet, and desktop without content overflow.
- Respect user preference for reduced motion.
